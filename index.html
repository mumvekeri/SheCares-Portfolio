Our Story

SheCares Charity, Non-Profit Organization

SheCares Charity is a beacon of hope, dedicated to supporting women's hygiene and menstrual health. As a non-profit organization, it provides essential menstrual donations, ensuring dignity and empowerment for women in need.

Our Mission

At our core, we are driven by a profound mission: to revolutionize menstrual hygiene. We believe every woman deserves dignity, comfort, and empowerment during her cycle. Through innovative programs and relentless advocacy, we strive to destigmatize menstruation, ensuring access to sustainable products and comprehensive education. Together, we're shaping a future where every woman's period is respected and celebrated.

Chidochashe Mumvekeri

Co-Founding Partner

Meet Chidochashe Mumvekeri, the visionary co-founding partner of SheCares Non-Profit Organization. With a heart as vast as her ambitions, Chidochashe is the driving force behind our mission to revolutionize menstrual hygiene. Her unwavering dedication and compassionate leadership inspire us to challenge taboos, advocate for change, and empower women worldwide. Chidochashe's commitment to equality and dignity fuels every initiative we undertake, propelling us towards a future where menstruation is met with respect and support. Join us in celebrating Chidochashe's tireless efforts and the transformative impact of SheCares under her guidance.

She believes that every individual has the right to access comprehensive menstrual health education, affordable hygiene products, and supportive community resources. Through SheCares, Chidochashe strives to break down barriers and create a world where menstruators are celebrated, not stigmatized. Her passion for social justice and gender equality drives her to advocate for policy reforms and systemic changes that prioritize menstrual health and well-being.
